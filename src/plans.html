Plans and progress

The project timeline is divided into 3 phases: Project Proposal, Prototype Development, and Final Reports. The first 5 weeks involved brainstorming the topic, devising functionalities, and preparing the documentation of the project. From week 5 onwards, the project will focus on structuring the layout and the design for the front-end of the website. From week 9 to week 12, we will concentrate on researching the business aspects of the project, finalizing the reports, and planning for post-production on a larger scale.

Project Proposal

In the first stage of the project, we were asked to devise a topic and write a project proposal from it. Therefore, we spent the first 2 weeks brainstorming ideas and holding meetings to estimate their feasibility. Although there were some intriguing ideas such as Machine Learning or Artificial Intelligence, we realized that they were too ambitious and required extensive technical skills and chose Thinhâ€™s topic which is Financial Management App. This topic was both applicable and practical as all members used to have difficulties managing their budgets and team expenses. From week 3 onwards, we were assigned to write the proposal and do research on its further development before submitting it for early feedback. After receiving the lecturer's advice, the misunderstanding about the implementation of the project was clear and the previous schedule was rearranged. Then we held a meeting to revise the scope of the proposal and work together to come up with the deliverables of the project. Finally, we did some research to reinforce the foundation of our topic.
