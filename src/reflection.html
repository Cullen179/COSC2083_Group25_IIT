Nguyen Truong Thinh

As the project manager of the Plutus project, I feel accomplished with the progress we made in the 12 weeks. Overall, the team was able to deliver a functional prototype that met the project requirements and was of high quality. One of the key achievements was our ability to maintain collaboration and effective communication among team members despite our diverse backgrounds. For example, we held regular meetings and utilized tools such as version control system and code reviews to ensure that everyone was on the same page. However, one area of improvement for me was time management. Despite the team's efforts, we underestimated the complexity of the required technology and did not prepare contingencies plan well. This resulted in some delays and we had to work extra hours to meet the deadlines. In the future, I will make sure to have a better contingency plan in place, and to communicate the risks and challenges clearly to the team. Overall, I am proud of the team's effort and the work we have accomplished in the given time frame. I believe that the experience of this project will be beneficial for the team members in their future endeavors.
