Nguyen Truong Thinh

He has been fortunate to secure a position as a Junior Product Manager at a SaaS company in Vietnam where his team is building a business intelligence platform. As a product manager, it is essential to have strong communication and leadership skills, as well as the ability to analyze data and make informed decisions. In addition to these skills, he believes it is crucial for a product manager to have a strong foundation in information technology. Understanding the technical side of product development is essential for making informed decisions and effectively leading cross-functional teams. In the future, Thinh hopes to continue honing these skills and becomes a more capable product manager, building impactful tech products that make a difference.
