Data Scientist

To build an effective model of a financial management system, this project will require the assistance of a data scientist. Due to our inexperience in working with data, the current app will only provide a fundamental analysis of user expenditure. Thus, it will be advantageous to the project when a data scientist can provide more analytical recommendations about user spending trends, facilitating them to reach their financial goals. In addition, he/she is expected to collaborate with the Marketing and Sales team to support business strategies and growth.

Responsibilities

  • Manipulate raw dataset with SQL and Tableau
  • Design, build, and maintain data efficient model of visualization, and statistics to provide data-driven recommendations to users
  • Collect and analyze customer behaviors, and app usage to improve user experience
  • Work closely with Marketing and Sales team to support data-driven decision making in business approach

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Math or Computer Science
  • At least 3 years of SQL and relational database experience
  • Proficiency in Excel, Powerpoint and visualization tools such as Tableau and Qlik
  • Fluent in programming languages such as Python and R
  • Analytical and detail-oriented mindset
  • In-depth knowledge in finance and business industries
  • Good communication, interpersonal, and presentation skills

Marketing and sales

The marketing and sales department will be able to deliver the product's promotion toward potential stakeholders to impact the market industries and public awareness. The role requires him/her to collaborate with related departments such as data scientists to enhance the overall marketing engagement in Plutus web application. The potential candidate should have leadership, communication, research, and critical thinking skills. This task is necessary as we need to reach out to as many customers as possible to promote the product.

Responsibilities

  • Advertise new projects to the market and promote existing products
  • Research marketing opportunities and collaborate with other departments for implementing sales strategies and advertising plans
  • Gather, analyze market data, and carry out a report form as needed
  • Contact customers, answer all information related to the product, including price, advantages, suitability

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Sales & Marketing, Business Administration, Hotel and Restaurant Management or related field
  • At least 3 years experience in the marketing and sales area.
  • Proficiency in report writing and data analysis
  • Excellent communication and written skills in English and Vietnamese
  • Strong leadership and organizational skills

Product Manager

The project will require the expertise of a Product Manager to successfully develop and manage the product roadmap. The current business intelligence platform is in need of further development and expansion, and a Product Manager will be responsible for defining the product strategy and features, as well as collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure the successful execution and delivery of the product. In addition, he/she will be expected to continuously gather and analyze market and customer insights, as well as monitor and report on the performance of the product.

Responsibilities

  • Define and prioritize product features and requirements
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to execute on product development and delivery
  • Gather and analyze market and customer insights to inform product decisions
  • Monitor and report on the performance of the product

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business or related field
  • At least 3 years of experience in product management
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Experience with agile development methodologies
  • Knowledge of information technology and its role in product development is highly desired.
